FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common queries about our services

What documents do I need to bring for Aadhaar services?

For Aadhaar services, please bring any one of the following original documents: Passport, PAN Card, Driving License, Voter ID, or any government-issued photo ID. For address update, bring a recent utility bill or bank statement.

How long does it take to process passport applications?

We complete the online application and appointment booking within 30 minutes. The actual passport processing time varies based on type: Normal (4-6 weeks), Tatkal (1-2 weeks). We ensure all documentation is perfect to avoid delays.

Do you provide home services for senior citizens?

Yes! We offer premium home services for senior citizens and differently-abled individuals within Kumbakonam city limits. Services include document assistance, photo capture, form filling, and online applications. Contact us to schedule a home visit.

What are your charges for digital services?

Our service charges are transparent and affordable. Basic services start at 50, with most government applications costing 100-300 depending on complexity. We provide detailed cost breakdown before starting any service. No hidden charges!

Can I get same-day photo printing services?

Absolutely! We provide instant photo printing services for passport, visa, Aadhaar, and other official requirements. Photos are printed in 5 minutes with high-quality professional equipment meeting all government specifications.
